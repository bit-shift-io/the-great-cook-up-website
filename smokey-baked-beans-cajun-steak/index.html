Smokey Bake Beans with Cajun Steak

Serves: 4

Time to make: 30min

Ingredients

Baked Beans

Steak

Method

Baked Beans

  1. Preheat oven to max (~240)
  2. Add all ingredients except cheese in to a sauce pan
  3. Bring to boil and boil for ~5min
  4. Move to oven proof baking dish
  5. Add cheese on top
  6. Cook till bubbling & golden

Steak

  1. Rub in flavours
  2. Cook on high (we had to use about medium on our induction plate) turning every 1 min. Cook till yout happy with hit.

Notes

Original: Jamie's 15 minute meals, p84
