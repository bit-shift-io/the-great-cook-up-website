a:T43d,<h1>Beef Stroganoff</h1>
<p>serves 4</p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>500g good cut of beef - eg. rib eye, boneless sirloin, sirloin steak tips, beef tenderloin </li>
<li>1tsp salt</li>
<li>1/4 tsp allspice (less is best)</li>
<li>2Tb olive oil</li>
<li>50g butter/margarine</li>
<li>1 small brown onion thinly sliced</li>
<li>300g white mushrooms thinly sliced</li>
<li>1/2 cup white wine (or chinese cooking wine)</li>
<li>1Tb flour</li>
<li>1/2 cup beef stock</li>
<li>1 cup sour cream, yogurt or milk (300ml)</li>
</ul>
<h2>Optional Extras</h2>
<ul>
<li>1Tb american mustard</li>
<li>Frozen veg?</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>salt + allspice as marinade for 1hr</li>
<li>saute beef w oil on high heat</li>
<li>remove beef</li>
<li>butter + onion + mush till soft and golden</li>
<li>deglaze pan with wine, reduce by 50%</li>
<li>stir in flour and cook 1-2m</li>
<li>add stock</li>
<li>medium low heat then add mustard</li>
<li>stir in cream, yogurt or milk</li>
<li>add beef</li>
<li>simmer for 15-20m</li>
<li>serve on french fries, mash, rice, pasta or noodles</li>
</ol>
