Method

  1. Lay fingers in dish.
  2. Strain fruit salad, cover findgers in fruit.
  3. Make jelly, cool, pour over fruit and let set.
  4. Make "Thick" custard, cool, pour over set jelly.

  6. Whip cream, spread over custard.
  7. Crush flake over.
