Loaded Baked Potato

Serves: 4
Prep: 10 m
Cook: 40-60 min

Ingredients

  • 1 baked potato per person
  • sour cream
  • tomato sauce
  • sweet chilli
  • cheese
  • coleslaw
  • 4 bean mix
  • bacon
  • onion
  • pineapple chunks

    • Method

  • Coat potato in oil and sprinkle with salt.
  • Cook potatos unwrapped in oven/airfrier for 45-60m depending on size.
  • Dice bacon and onion. Cook till golden brown.
  • 4 bean mix with oil and balsamic.
