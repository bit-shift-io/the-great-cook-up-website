Lemonade

Time: 10 min prep + 2 hr fridge

Ingredients

Method

  1. Stir boiling water and sugar together in heatproof jug. Set aside to cool.
  2. Juice lemons and mix in (strain if you like).
  3. Add 2 cups (for strong), or 3 cups (for mild) flavour.
  4. Refrigate.

Notes

Tates good when run through soda stream.
