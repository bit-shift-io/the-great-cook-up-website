a:T54c,<h1>Chicken and Mushroom One-pot</h1>
<p>Serves: 16</p>
<p>Time: 1.5hr prep and cook</p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>2kg chicken thigh</li>
<li>600g button mushrooms</li>
<li>6 large celery sticks (or just 1 whole bunch)</li>
<li>3x red onions (we used 8x shallots)</li>
<li>4Tbs garlic</li>
<li>2Tbs tomato paste</li>
<li>2tea chilli</li>
<li>2Tbs thyme</li>
<li>2Tbs parsley</li>
<li>250ml apple cider vinegar (or white wine)</li>
<li>2x400g can diced tomato</li>
<li>370ml chicken stock</li>
<li>Serve with couscous, rice or mashed potato</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>Cut up chicken, mushrooms, celery, onions. Defrost frozen veg. Prepare chicken stock</li>
<li>Brown chicken in large wok then remove </li>
<li>Brown mushrooms for ~5min or till golden. Add celery, onion, garlic and frozen veg. Cook ~5min or till soft, stir occasionally.</li>
<li>Add tomato paste, vinegar and cook for ~2min, constantly stirring.</li>
<li>Add tomato, stock, chilli, parsly, thyme, chicken. Bring to boil. Simmer ~15min.</li>
</ol>
<p>Serve with couscous, rice or mashed potato</p>
<h2>Notes</h2>
<ul>
<li>Original recipe: <a href="https://www.taste.com.au/recipes/low-cal-chicken-mushroom-one-pot/clnk7ezq">https://www.taste.com.au/recipes/low-cal-chicken-mushroom-one-pot/clnk7ezq</a></li>
</ul>
