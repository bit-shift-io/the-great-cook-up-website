Carrot Oat Cake

Serves 8

Wet Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup (120g) melted butter
  • 1 cup (285g) greek yogurt
  • 3/4 cup (255g) honey
  • 1tsp vanilla essence
  • 2 cups (200g or 2 carrots) grated carrot

    • Dry Ingredients

  • 3/4-1 cup (65g) walnuts
  • 2tsp cinnamon
  • 1tsp baking soda
  • 1tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup (90g) quick oats
  • 1.5 cups (150g) oat flour/white flour

    • Method

  • Mix wet, add dry and use a stick mixer
  • 180c ~60m cover with foil for first half

    • Notes
