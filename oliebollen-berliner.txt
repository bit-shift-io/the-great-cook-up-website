a:T50c,<h1>Oliebollen/Berliner - Dutch Oil Balls</h1>
<p>Serves 10</p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>4 cups flour</li>
<li>2 cup milk/buttermilk</li>
<li>4Tb butter (if using milk)</li>
<li>3tsp yeast</li>
<li>2Tb sugar</li>
<li>1tsp salt</li>
<li>2 eggs</li>
</ul>
<h2>Extras</h2>
<ul>
<li>1/2 cup raisins (soaked in water for 15m)</li>
<li>1-2 apples</li>
<li>2tsp cinnamon/mixed spice</li>
<li>powder with icing sugar</li>
<li>inject warm jam</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>Warm milk and mix with sugar, yeast beaten egg and salt</li>
<li>Add flour and mix</li>
<li>Add extras</li>
<li>Proof for 1hr if making oliebollen</li>
<li>If making berliner kneed, reduce milk slightly, and cut into balls, rise 2hr</li>
<li>Use 2 oil diped spoons to shape into balls and deep fry 180C for about 3min in saucepan ~1L oil</li>
</ol>
