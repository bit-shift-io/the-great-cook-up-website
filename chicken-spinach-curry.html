Chicken Spinach Curry

Serves: 12

Time: 30min prep + 45min cook

Ingredients

  • 1.5kg chicken
  • 1kg frozen veg
  • 2x brown onions
  • 280g baby spinach (orig recipe suggests 150g)
  • 4tsp garlic
  • 4tsp ginger
  • 4tsp cumin
  • 4tsp corriander flakes (should have been 2tsp)
  • 2tsp tumeric
  • 4tsp chilli flakes/sriracha
  • 4tbsp lime juice
  • 4tbsp fish sauce

    • Method

  • Dice chicken and onion. Defrost frozen veg. Cut spinach up. Mix sauce ingredients.
  • Brown chicken then remove from wok.
  • Brown onion.
  • Throw in everything except the spinach. Simmer for 15min. stirring occasionaly.
  • Add spinach, stir till wilted, then simmer for another 5min with lid off.

    • Notes
