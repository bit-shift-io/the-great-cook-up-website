a:T414,<h1>Potato and Leek Soup</h1>
<p>Serves: 14</p>
<p>Time: 20min prep + 60min cook</p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>2x brown onion</li>
<li>2tsp garlic</li>
<li>2kg potatos</li>
<li>4 leeks, thinly sliced, pale section only</li>
<li>2.5L veg stock</li>
<li>300ml light sour cream</li>
<li>500g bacon, diced</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>Dice bacon, onion. Peel and dice potatos. Thinly slice leek (pale part only). Prepare stock</li>
<li>Brown onion, garlic, bacon in large woke or pot</li>
<li>Add potato and leek, cook for 5min or till leek softens</li>
<li>Add stock and boil gently for 30min or till potato softens. Cool for 10min</li>
<li>Blend smoothish</li>
<li>On medium heat add cream. Stir and cook for 5min or till hot. Season with salt</li>
</ol>
<p>Serve with toast and chives</p>
<h2>Notes</h2>
<ul>
<li>Origional recipe: <a href="https://www.taste.com.au/recipes/potato-leek-soup-2/56c16a16-d92c-475c-92cc-4b8eecd6c0c5">https://www.taste.com.au/recipes/potato-leek-soup-2/56c16a16-d92c-475c-92cc-4b8eecd6c0c5</a></li>
</ul>
