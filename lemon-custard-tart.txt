a:T490,<h1>Lemon Custard Tart</h1>
<p>serves 10</p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>1 L milk</li>
<li>6 Tb sugar</li>
<li>1 tsp vanilla essence</li>
<li>1 lemon zest</li>
<li>1-2 lemon juice</li>
<li>2-3 eggs (try 3 eggs next time)</li>
<li>1/2 tsp salt</li>
<li>6 Tb custard or cornstarch</li>
</ul>
<h2>Base</h2>
<ul>
<li>200 g dry buscuits</li>
<li>80-100 g melted butter</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>Grind the biscuit add the butter and pat into pan</li>
<li>Mix milk, sugar, vanilla and lemon zest, then heat over medium heat</li>
<li>Mix eggs with the custard powder and salt</li>
<li>When the milk is hot, lower the heat to low and add the egg mixture slowly while stirring</li>
<li>Cook over low heat without stopping stirring until the mixture becomes thick</li>
<li>Remove from the heat</li>
<li>Add the lemon juice then pour over the base</li>
<li>Put in the refrigerator for 2 to 4 hours</li>
</ol>
<h2>Notes</h2>
<p>Next time try 3 eggs for a more solid cake.
If you skip the base, and make small serving, this makes a good warm lemon custard!</p>
