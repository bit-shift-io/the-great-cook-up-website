Portuguese Chicken Bake

Serves: 14 Time to make: 30min prep/pre-cook + 1hr bake

Ingredients

  • 2cup rice (we used borwn rice)
  • 3tbs portuguese spice
  • 1tsp parsley
  • 4tsp brown sugar
  • 2 lemons juiced + rind
  • 1.5kg chicken legs (next time we want to use 2kg)
  • 2 brown onion, diced
  • 4tsp garlic
  • 2 red capsicums, diced
  • 500g frozen corn kernels
  • 2x400g can diced tomato
  • 2cup chicken stock

    • Method

  • Cut onion, capy, rind n squeeze lemon
  • Preheat oven to 200deg. Grease baking dishes. Combine portuguese spice, parley, sugar, lemon and rind in a large bowl. Add chicken and coat well in mixture.
  • In a large woke/frying pan, medium heat oil. Cook chicken in batches till golden borwn (~5min)
  • Add oil, onion, garlic and remaining spice mix to frying pan for ~6min till soft
  • Add capy, corn, rice, tomatoes and stock. Bring to boil. Pour into baking dish
  • Add chicken on top and press into rice mix a little. Cover with foil. Bake for 25min
  • Remove foil, bake for 30min

    • Notes

    I think a dolop of yoghourt/sour cream would go well with this
