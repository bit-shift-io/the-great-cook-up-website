Rocky Road

Serves: 12

Time: 20min prep + 2hr sets

Ingredients

Method

  1. Quarter marshmellows and lollies, roughly chop nuts.
  2. Line a 20cm baking dish with baking paper.
  3. Melt chocolate with oil: Microwave for 30s increments, stiring till just melted.
  4. Stir in add-ins.
  5. Refrigerate 2hr.

Notes

Other add-ins to try: shortbread, potato chips, spearmint lollies, glace cherries. To try next time: swap amount of dark and milk. melt choc straight in baking dish.
