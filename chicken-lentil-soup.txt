a:T55d,<h1>Chicken Lentil Soup</h1>
<p>Serves: 14</p>
<p>Time: 30min prep + 1hr cook</p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li><p>0.5kg chicken (try 1kg next time)</p>
</li>
<li><p>2x Onion</p>
</li>
<li><p>1kg frozen veg</p>
</li>
<li><p>4tsp garlic paste</p>
</li>
<li><p>2tsp oregano</p>
</li>
<li><p>2tsp basil</p>
</li>
<li><p>2x bay leaf (optional)</p>
</li>
<li><p>800g crushed tomato</p>
</li>
<li><p>800g lentils (I used canned, but should try with dry lentils as per origional recipe)</p>
</li>
<li><p>4Tb Vinegar</p>
</li>
<li><p>2cup water (should be 10 cups if following origional recipe ratios, but too watery!)</p>
</li>
<li><p>6cup chicken stock</p>
</li>
<li><p>1cup Spinach (optional)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>Dice and brown chicken</li>
<li>Dice and brown onion, frozen veg, garlic paste, oregano, basil, bay leaf</li>
<li>Combine chicken with other mixture</li>
<li>Add crushed tomato, lentils, vinegar, stock and water</li>
<li>Simmer for 1hr, stir occasionally. Add salt and paper + extra vinegar to flavour</li>
<li>Add spinach and cook until wilts</li>
</ol>
<h2>Notes</h2>
<ul>
<li>Origional recipe: <a href="https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/13978/lentil-soup/">https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/13978/lentil-soup/</a></li>
</ul>
