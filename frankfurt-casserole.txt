a:T437,<h1>Frankfurt Casserole</h1>
<p>Serves: 6</p>
<p>Time: 15 min prep + 20 min cook</p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>375g packet of Pommes Noisettes</li>
<li>30g marg/butter</li>
<li>2 Tbl plain flour</li>
<li>425g tomato juice (We used diced tomato, alternate sauces might also be good)</li>
<li>500g chunk frozen veg (We didn&#39;t defrest these but it might be better)</li>
<li>1 sachet beef cup-a-soup (2 sachets might be better?)</li>
<li>1/2 cup water (we ended up with 1 cup due to the frozen veg)</li>
<li>500g frankfurts, sliced</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>Slice frankfurts. Preheat oven to 230 degrees. Defrost veg? Prepare noisettes on baking tray with baking paper (Maybe sprinkle chippy/chicken salt over)</li>
<li>Melt butter, add flour.</li>
<li>Add tomato juice, vegetables, soup mix, water, frankfurts. Bring to boil, then simmer for 20 min.</li>
<li>Pop in the Noisettes as these take ~15 mins as per packet instructions.</li>
<li>Mix noisettes in to sauce veg mix.</li>
</ol>
<h2>Notes</h2>
<p>Original: Womans weekly menu planner No.? Series ?. p14</p>
