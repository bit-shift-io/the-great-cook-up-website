Bibimbap - Korean Rice Bowl

Serves 2-4

Marinade

  • 500g thin sliced beef
  • 3 Tb soy
  • 2 tsp ginger paste
  • 2 tsp garlic paste
  • 2 tsp brown sugar
  • 2 tsp warm water

Ingredients

Method

  1. Slice your beef into thin pieces. Marinade
  2. Cook other veg + rice/noodles
  3. Consume
