Frankfurt Casserole

Serves: 6

Time: 15 min prep + 20 min cook

Ingredients

  • 375g packet of Pommes Noisettes
  • 30g marg/butter
  • 2 Tbl plain flour
  • 425g tomato juice (We used diced tomato, alternate sauces might also be good)
  • 500g chunk frozen veg (We didn't defrest these but it might be better)
  • 1 sachet beef cup-a-soup (2 sachets might be better?)
  • 1/2 cup water (we ended up with 1 cup due to the frozen veg)
  • 500g frankfurts, sliced

    • Method

  • Slice frankfurts. Preheat oven to 230 degrees. Defrost veg? Prepare noisettes on baking tray with baking paper (Maybe sprinkle chippy/chicken salt over)
  • Melt butter, add flour.
  • Add tomato juice, vegetables, soup mix, water, frankfurts. Bring to boil, then simmer for 20 min.
  • Pop in the Noisettes as these take ~15 mins as per packet instructions.
  • Mix noisettes in to sauce veg mix.
