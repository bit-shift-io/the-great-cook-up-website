Carbonara

Serves 4

Ingredients

  • bacon
  • egg
  • spagetti
  • 1-2 onion
  • greek yogurt
  • starch water from the pasta cooking
  • oregano/italian herb mix etc

    • Method

  • Cook pasta in salted water (10g per litre)
  • Fry bacon in deep pan with garlic/ginger
  • To the side add eggs and greek yogurt and mix
  • When pasta is done, add to the bacon pan with the bacon fat and coat pasta in fat
  • Finally turn off heat and add the egg mix and allow to passive cook
  • Top with cheese

    • Notes

    Dont rinse pasta when cooked, allow it to cool a little to become sticky
