a:T4da,<h1>Cauliflower Soup</h1>
<p>Serves: 4</p>
<p>Time: 15min prep + 55min cook</p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>1 whole cauliflower</li>
<li>1 brown onion</li>
<li>4 tsp garlic paste</li>
<li>3-4 cups chicken stock</li>
<li>400g can cannellini beans</li>
<li>500g bacon</li>
<li>1 cups mixed veg</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li><p>Preheat over to 190deg. Cut cauliflower into small florets. Cut onion into slices. Drain and rinse beans</p>
</li>
<li><p>Spray oven tray live oil. Put on cauliflower (leave 1/2 cup aside) and onion. Roast for 30min</p>
</li>
<li><p>Fry onion, bacon, garlic and veg in wok. then put aside</p>
</li>
<li><p>Put roast veg into wok. Add stock beans. Bring to simmer. Cook for ~15min or till cauliflower tender</p>
</li>
<li><p>Remove from heat. Blend a little leaving chunky. Add remaining cauliflower</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h2>Notes</h2>
<p>Tastes very similar to potato and leek soup</p>
<p>Original recipe also uses rosemary and chilli oil which might add more flavour</p>
