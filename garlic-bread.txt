a:T48e,<h1>Garlic Butter</h1>
<p>Serves 8</p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>1 baguette bread</li>
<li>1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil</li>
<li>1/4 cup margarine</li>
<li>3-4 cloves garlic</li>
<li>2 tbsp freshly chopped parsley or coriander</li>
<li>pinch salt</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>Cut a baguette into diagonal slices that are 1-inch thick</li>
<li>Add a 1/4 cup of extra virgin olive oil into a large bowl, also add in a 1/4 cup of margarine, finely shred in 3 cloves of garlic and 2 tbsp of freshly chopped parsley, whisk together until well combined</li>
<li>Evenly spread the mixture on each piece of baguette bread, then add the breads into a baking tray lined with foil paper and season each bread with sea salt</li>
<li>Add into a preheated oven, bake option 250 C for exactly 3 minutes, remove from the oven and serve, enjoy! (or 200C for 8-10 mins)</li>
</ol>
<h2>Notes</h2>
<p>If you want to use butter, replace margarine + oil mix with 1/2 cup of softened butter</p>
