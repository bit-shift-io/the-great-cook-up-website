Cheesecake

Time: 20 min + 6 hrs set

Serves: 12

Crust Ingredients

Filling Ingredients

Method

  1. Blitz crumb ingredients. Press into a pan.
  2. Whip cream in a bowl.
  3. Whip cream cheese, sugar, vanilla and lemon, salt, gelatine in a seperate bowl.
  4. Fold in cream.
  5. Spread over crust, then fridge for ~6 hrs.

Notes

Decorate with passionfruit pulp or strawberries. Gelatine stuffed up and made it gross, so try without! Try do a thin crust and thin layer of cake over the top - maybe a pizza tray with baking paper?
