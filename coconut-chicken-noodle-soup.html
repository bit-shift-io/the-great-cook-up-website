Coconut Chicken Noodle Soup

Serves: 4

Time: 10 min prep + 15 min cook

Ingredients

Method

  1. Dice pumpkin (smaller is better), Slice capsicum, carrot, broccolini.
  2. Add all ingredients into frying pan or casserole pot.
  3. Cover pot and bring to boil over high heat. Cook for 10-15 min, or till vegies are tender.

Notes

  • Serve with peanuts, fried shallots or a squeeze of lime juice.
