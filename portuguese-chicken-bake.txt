a:T605,<h1>Portuguese Chicken Bake</h1>
<p>Serves: 14
Time to make: 30min prep/pre-cook + 1hr bake</p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>2cup rice (we used borwn rice)</li>
<li>3tbs portuguese spice</li>
<li>1tsp parsley</li>
<li>4tsp brown sugar</li>
<li>2 lemons juiced + rind</li>
<li>1.5kg chicken legs (next time we want to use 2kg)</li>
<li>2 brown onion, diced</li>
<li>4tsp garlic</li>
<li>2 red capsicums, diced</li>
<li>500g frozen corn kernels</li>
<li>2x400g can diced tomato</li>
<li>2cup chicken stock</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li><p>Cut onion, capy, rind n squeeze lemon</p>
</li>
<li><p>Preheat oven to 200deg. Grease baking dishes. Combine portuguese spice, parley, sugar, lemon and rind in a large bowl. Add chicken and coat well in mixture.</p>
</li>
<li><p>In a large woke/frying pan, medium heat oil. Cook chicken in batches till golden borwn (~5min)</p>
</li>
<li><p>Add oil, onion, garlic and remaining spice mix to frying pan for ~6min till soft</p>
</li>
<li><p>Add capy, corn, rice, tomatoes and stock. Bring to boil. Pour into baking dish</p>
</li>
<li><p>Add chicken on top and press into rice mix a little. Cover with foil. Bake for 25min</p>
</li>
<li><p>Remove foil, bake for 30min</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h2>Notes</h2>
<p>I think a dolop of yoghourt/sour cream would go well with this</p>
