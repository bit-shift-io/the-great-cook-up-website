Pumpkin Scones

Makes 12

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups sr flour
  • pinch salt
  • 1Tb butter
  • 2Tb sugar
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup mashed pumpkin

Method

  1. cream butter and sugar, add egg and mix
  2. fold in pumpkin
  3. add flour + milk
  4. kneed well
  5. roll dough and cut to size
  6. bake 180c 15-20mins

Notes
