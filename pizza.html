Pizza

Makes 1 tray Prep 2 hr Cook 30 min

Dough

  • 2 cups plain flour (not bread flour) (try 1-1.5 cups as its to thick)
  • 250 ml water (add more if needed)
  • 2 1/4 tsp yeast
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp olive oil

Ingredients

Method

  1. 1st proof 1 hr or double size
  2. fit to tray with flour + oil
  3. 2nd proof 30-45m
  4. heat oven fully to 220-250c
  5. 3-4m till slightly changing color
  6. add toppings
  7. cook ~16m till golden

Notes

Use baking paper + flour on tray Should be a ragged slightly damp dough Easy on the toppings Drain topings well
