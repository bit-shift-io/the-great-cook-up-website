Curried Sausages

Serves 4 Prep 10m Cook 30-40m

Ingredients

Extras

Method

  1. Set rice machine going...
  2. Medium-high heat, cook sausages, turning to brown all over then remove and chop, keep seperate.
  3. Cook potato, carrot, cellery and extra veggies till brown and soft about 20-30m.
  4. Add garlic and onion. Cook for 2m until translucent.
  5. Add curry powder + flour and stir for 30s.
  6. Pour in chicken stock, sugar and pepper, mixing constantly.
  7. Add sausages and peas, bring to simmer and cook for 5-10m or until sauce thickens.
  8. Serve over mashed potato, rice, pasta or noodles. For low carb, try mashed cauliflower!
