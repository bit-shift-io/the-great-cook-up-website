a:T5b1,<h1>Garlic Alfredo Sauce</h1>
<p>2.5 cups of sauce</p>
<p>For use on pasta or chicken shnitz</p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>1/4 cup (113 g) butter (1/2 stick)</li>
<li>4 cloves (4 cloves) garlic , minced (or 2 teaspoons garlic powder)</li>
<li>1/4 cup (30 g) all-purpose flour</li>
<li>2 cups (480 ml) milk</li>
<li>1/4 teaspoon (1.25 ml) dried herbs (basil, oregano, thyme, etc.)</li>
<li>1/2 teaspoon (2.5 ml) dried mustard powder</li>
<li>1 cup (100 g) grated Parmesan cheese</li>
<li>1 teaspoon (5 ml) salt , or to taste</li>
<li>1/2 teaspoon (2.5 ml) fresh cracked black pepper</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>Heat a sauce pan on medium-high heat. Melt butter, then add garlic and cook translucent and fragrant (about 1 minute). Whisk in flour and continue whisking to remove large clumps.</li>
<li>Slowly whisk in the milk. Whisking often to make sure flour lumps are smooth, bring sauce to a low boil. Then reduce heat to low.</li>
<li>Add dried herbs, mustard powder, parmesan, salt and pepper. Continue whisking until sauce is smooth and begins to thicken.</li>
<li>If you’re saving the sauce for later, allow the sauce to completely cool.</li>
<li>Keep in fridge and use within 5 days.</li>
</ol>
<h2>Notes</h2>
<p>If you want to use heavy cream, then just eliminate the flour and replace the milk with heavy cream. </p>
