a:T607,<h1>Sri Lankan Chicken Curry</h1>
<p>Serves: 12</p>
<p>Time to make: 30min prep, 4hr cook</p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>4tsp macadamia oil (or cooking oil)</li>
<li>1.2kg chicken thigh, diced</li>
<li>2 onion</li>
<li>4tsp ginger paste or powder</li>
<li>4tsp cumin</li>
<li>4tsp coriander</li>
<li>2tsp turmeric</li>
<li>2 cinammon sticks</li>
<li>2 cup chicken stock (make double strength)</li>
<li>400ml coconut milk</li>
<li>4tsp tamarind paste</li>
<li>2tsp brown sugar</li>
<li>2 red chillis</li>
<li>900g sweet potato</li>
<li>600g cauliflower (I used a 500g frozen bag, maybe half a fresh cauli?)</li>
<li>4tsp cornflour (optional)</li>
<li>250g snow peas</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li><p>Dice chicken, dice onion, dice chilli, dice sweet potato, dice snow peas. Prep chicken stock.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Brown chicken in large wok/frying pan. Add onion and brown.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Add ginger, cumin, coriander, turmeric, cinnamon. Cook for 2 min or till aromatic.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Add chicken stock, coconut milk, taarind, sugar and chilli. Bring to boil then transfer to slow cooker.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Add sweet potato, cauliflower to slow cooker and cook on high for 3.5hr.</p>
</li>
<li><p>Add cornflour, add snow peas. Cook for 30min.</p>
</li>
</ol>

</ul>
