a:T603,<h1>Schnitzel</h1>
<p>Serves as many as you make</p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>4 chicken, pork, beef</li>
<li>8 cups of water for brine</li>
<li>1/3 cup salt for brine</li>
<li>1 cup plain flour</li>
<li>salt + pepper</li>
<li>2 beatten eggs</li>
<li>panko crumbs</li>
</ul>
<h2>Extras</h2>
<ul>
<li>cornflour to make crispy</li>
<li>pasta sauce + cheese for parmigiana</li>
<li>chipotle powder, galic salt, tomato powder to initial flour mix</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>Cut to fingers or butterfly if too thick</li>
<li>Brine in water + salt 15-30m</li>
<li>Dry off meat then use mallet to make even thickness if not making fingers</li>
<li>Place on platter or plate, or throw into plastic bag and shake</li>
<li>Sprinkle salt, pepper + flour over and pat into the meat</li>
<li>Wipe off excess flour from plate</li>
<li>Beat eggs and pour/brush over the meat, ensure even coating</li>
<li>Bread crumb mix in a sparate plate/bowl</li>
<li>Refridgerate for 10-20m to dry</li>
<li>Fry in pan with 1-2cm of olive oil/butter mix, or airfry/bake</li>
</ol>
