a:T406,<h1>Beef Kofta Curry</h1>
<p>Serves: 6</p>
<p>Time to make: 30min</p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li><p>250g lentils</p>
</li>
<li><p>1 heaped tsp garam masala</p>
</li>
<li><p>400g beef mince</p>
</li>
<li><p>3 tomatoes</p>
</li>
<li><p>1tsp ginger</p>
</li>
<li><p>2 spring onions</p>
</li>
<li><p>2 chilli&#39;s (squirt of siracha would be good to try also)</p>
</li>
<li><p>1tsp tumeric</p>
</li>
<li><p>1tsp honey</p>
</li>
<li><p>2 heaped tsp Rogan josh paste (needs more, try 2 Tbl)</p>
</li>
<li><p>200g light coconut milk</p>
</li>
<li><p>serve with dolop of natural/greek yoghurt</p>
</li>
<li><p>serve with rice</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>Dice tomatoes &amp; spring onions &amp; chilli, add ginger, honey, tumeric, curry paste and cocounut milk into blender then blend.</li>
<li>Mix lentils, garam masala, mince, pepper and salt. Brown in wok.</li>
<li>Add paste from #1 into wok.</li>
<li>Bring to boil then simmer.</li>
</ol>
<h2>Notes</h2>
<ul>
<li>Original: Jamie&#39;s 15 minute meals, p66</li>
</ul>
