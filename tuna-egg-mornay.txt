a:T4cc,<h1>Tuna &amp; Egg Mornay</h1>
<p>Serves: 6</p>
<p>Time to make: 1hr prep + precook, 30min cook</p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>4 eggs</li>
<li>1 onion</li>
<li>1 green capsicum (or frozen veg)</li>
<li>50g butter (~1/4 cup)</li>
<li>50g plain flour</li>
<li>1/2 litre of milk</li>
<li>3 Tbl grated cheese</li>
<li>425g can tuna</li>
<li>salt &amp; pepper</li>
</ul>
<h3>Topping</h3>
<ul>
<li>1.5 cups breadcrumbs</li>
<li>30g melted butter</li>
<li>1/2 cup grated cheese (need more, maybe 1 cup?)</li>
<li>1 Tbl fresh or dried parsley</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>Boil eggs (4min from putting kettle water in, rest for 4min in water) and coursely dice, dice onion and cappy. Drain tuna - set aside juice.</li>
<li>Prepare topping mixture</li>
<li>Melt butter, add onion and cappy. Fry till tender</li>
<li>Add flour and gradually stir in milk (off heat). Bring to boil and stir until tickened. Keep mixing!</li>
<li>Add tuna, ~50% of tuna juice, egg, cheese, salt &amp; paper. Heat through.</li>
<li>Pour into 2 litre baking dish. Sprinkle with topping</li>
<li>Bake for ~30min @ 180 deg</li>
</ol>
<h2>Notes</h2>
<ul>
<li>500g shredded cheese pack should be ample</li>
<li>Original: Rayelenes&#39; Recipes</li>
</ul>
