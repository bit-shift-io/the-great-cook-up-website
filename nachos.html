Nachos

Serves: 24

Time: 20min prep + 20min cook

Ingredients

  • 1kg corn tortillas, cut into wedges
  • 700g mince
  • 4x red onions, finely chopped
  • 8tsp garlic
  • 4tsp chilli
  • 4tsp paprika
  • 4tsp cumin
  • 4x red capsicum, diced
  • 4x zuchini, diced
  • 4x 400g cans of black beans, drain & rinse
  • 4x 400g cans of diced tomato
  • 1 small bowl frozen veg, defrosted
  • 300ml water
  • squirt of lime (optional)
  • 2tsp corriander (optional)
  • 500g natural yoghurt or sour cream (optional but recommended)

    • Method

  • Preheat oven to 200 degrees, cut tortillas into wedges and put on baking paper. Lightly coat in oil. Bake ~10mins, turning once or until golden and crisp
  • Brown mince in saucepan
  • Add onion, garlic, chilli, paprika, capsicum, zuchini, corridander in saucepan on medium heat with some oil till softened
  • Add black beans, tomatoes, frozen veg, water, squirt of lime. Bring to boil then simmer for 10min or until thickened
  • Serve bean mix with tortilla crisps. Top with optional yoghurt, fresh coriander, lime, paprika

    • Notes

  • Sauce can be cooked and frozen then just prepare corn wedges on the day you want to eat
  • Should be amazing with some cheese on top
