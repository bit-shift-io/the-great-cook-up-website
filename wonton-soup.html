Wonton Soup

Serves 3

Base Ingredients

  • 1tsp ginger powder
  • 1tsp garlic powder
  • 1Tb cooking wine
  • 1/2Tb oyster sauce
  • 2Tb fish sauce
  • 1/2Tb seasame oil
  • 1/2Tb soy sauce

    • Ingredients

  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 1-2 chicken breasts
  • 10-15 wontons
  • buk choy quatered lengthwise
  • egg noddles
  • spring onion
  • bean sprouts

    • Extras

  • grated carrot
  • spinach instead of bok choy
  • mushrooms
  • shrimp

    • Method

  • Fry chicken and put to side
  • Add sauce ingredients and fry for 20s
  • Add stock and bring to boil
  • Add chicken, noodles, wontons
  • Add buk choy
  • Sprinkle w spring onions

    • Notes

    Add 1/2 cup water to dilute the stock if too salty
