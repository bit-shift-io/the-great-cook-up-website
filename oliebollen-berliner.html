Oliebollen/Berliner - Dutch Oil Balls

Serves 10

Ingredients

  • 4 cups flour
  • 2 cup milk/buttermilk
  • 4Tb butter (if using milk)
  • 3tsp yeast
  • 2Tb sugar
  • 1tsp salt
  • 2 eggs

    • Extras

  • 1/2 cup raisins (soaked in water for 15m)
  • 1-2 apples
  • 2tsp cinnamon/mixed spice
  • powder with icing sugar
  • inject warm jam

    • Method

  • Warm milk and mix with sugar, yeast beaten egg and salt
  • Add flour and mix
  • Add extras
  • Proof for 1hr if making oliebollen
  • If making berliner kneed, reduce milk slightly, and cut into balls, rise 2hr
  • Use 2 oil diped spoons to shape into balls and deep fry 180C for about 3min in saucepan ~1L oil
