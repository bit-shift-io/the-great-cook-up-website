a:T4de,<h1>Curried Sausages</h1>
<p>Serves 4<br>Prep 10m<br>Cook 30-40m  </p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>600 g sausages</li>
<li>4 small potatos small cubed</li>
<li>2-3 cellery diced</li>
<li>1-2 carrots diced</li>
<li>2 garlic cloves</li>
<li>1 onion sliced</li>
<li>1 TB curry powder</li>
<li>2 TB flour</li>
<li>2 cups chicken stock</li>
<li>1 tsp sugar</li>
<li>1/2 tsp pepper</li>
<li>1 cup peas</li>
</ul>
<h2>Extras</h2>
<ul>
<li>cauliflower</li>
<li>zucchini</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol start="0">
<li>Set rice machine going...</li>
<li>Medium-high heat, cook sausages, turning to brown all over then remove and chop, keep seperate.</li>
<li>Cook potato, carrot, cellery and extra veggies till brown and soft about 20-30m.</li>
<li>Add garlic and onion. Cook for 2m until translucent.</li>
<li>Add curry powder + flour and stir for 30s.</li>
<li>Pour in chicken stock, sugar and pepper, mixing constantly.</li>
<li>Add sausages and peas, bring to simmer and cook for 5-10m or until sauce thickens.</li>
<li>Serve over mashed potato, rice, pasta or noodles. For low carb, try mashed cauliflower!</li>
</ol>
<h2>Notes</h2>
