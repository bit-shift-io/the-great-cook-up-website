Cauliflower Soup

Serves: 4

Time: 15min prep + 55min cook

Ingredients

  • 1 whole cauliflower
  • 1 brown onion
  • 4 tsp garlic paste
  • 3-4 cups chicken stock
  • 400g can cannellini beans
  • 500g bacon
  • 1 cups mixed veg

    • Method

  • Preheat over to 190deg. Cut cauliflower into small florets. Cut onion into slices. Drain and rinse beans
  • Spray oven tray live oil. Put on cauliflower (leave 1/2 cup aside) and onion. Roast for 30min
  • Fry onion, bacon, garlic and veg in wok. then put aside
  • Put roast veg into wok. Add stock beans. Bring to simmer. Cook for ~15min or till cauliflower tender
  • Remove from heat. Blend a little leaving chunky. Add remaining cauliflower

    • Notes

    Tastes very similar to potato and leek soup

    Original recipe also uses rosemary and chilli oil which might add more flavour
