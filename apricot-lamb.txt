a:T543,<h1>Apricot Lamb</h1>
<p>Serves 14</p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>1.5kg lamb leg roast (boneless)</li>
<li>2 brown onion</li>
<li>4 celery sticks</li>
<li>6 large carrots (try frozen veg instead ~1kg?)</li>
<li>4tsp ground cummin</li>
<li>4tsp ground corrinder</li>
<li>1l (4 cups) chicken stock)</li>
<li>1 squeezed lemon</li>
<li>1Tbs honey</li>
<li>2Tbs tomato paste</li>
<li>2x400g can chickpeas</li>
<li>200g dried apricots</li>
<li>2x cinnamon sticks</li>
<li>1 can corn kernels (optional)</li>
<li>mint sauce to serve</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>Dice lamb into ~1.5cm cubes. Dice onions, finely slice celery. Slice carrots. Drain chickpeas. Prep chicken stock. Dice apricots.</li>
<li>Brown lamb medium-high and add to slow cooker.</li>
<li>Medium heat the onion, celery, carrot, cumin, corriander, cinamon for about 5 minutes. Transfer to slow cooker.</li>
<li>Add stock, lemon, honey tomato paste to slow cooker.</li>
<li>Cook on high for ~3.5hrs</li>
<li>Add chickpeas, apricots + corn kernels</li>
<li>Cook for 30 minutes.</li>
</ol>
<p>Serve with mint sauce</p>
<h2>Notes</h2>
