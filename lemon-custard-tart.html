Lemon Custard Tart

serves 10

Ingredients

  • 1 L milk
  • 6 Tb sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1 lemon zest
  • 1-2 lemon juice
  • 2-3 eggs (try 3 eggs next time)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 6 Tb custard or cornstarch

    • Base

  • 200 g dry buscuits
  • 80-100 g melted butter

    • Method

  • Grind the biscuit add the butter and pat into pan
  • Mix milk, sugar, vanilla and lemon zest, then heat over medium heat
  • Mix eggs with the custard powder and salt
  • When the milk is hot, lower the heat to low and add the egg mixture slowly while stirring
  • Cook over low heat without stopping stirring until the mixture becomes thick
  • Remove from the heat
  • Add the lemon juice then pour over the base
  • Put in the refrigerator for 2 to 4 hours

    • Notes

    Next time try 3 eggs for a more solid cake. If you skip the base, and make small serving, this makes a good warm lemon custard!
