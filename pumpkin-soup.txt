a:T440,<h1>Pumpkin Soup</h1>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>1 TB butter</li>
<li>1 TB olive oil</li>
<li>1 onion chopped</li>
<li>2 garlic cloves</li>
<li>750g peeled cubed pumpkin</li>
<li>4 cups chicken stock</li>
<li>salt pepper to taste</li>
</ul>
<h2>Extras</h2>
<ul>
<li>cream</li>
<li>yogurt</li>
<li>chives</li>
<li>potato</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>Heat oil in a large pot and cook onion and garlic over low heat until onion is translucent.</li>
<li>Add pumpkin, potato and stock and bring to the boil.</li>
<li>Reduce heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until pumpkin is tender.</li>
<li>Remove from heat and cool slightly.</li>
<li>Puree stick blender until smooth.</li>
<li>Add yoghurt/cream to serve.</li>
</ol>
<p>Tips: If the soup is too thick add a little low-fat milk to make desired consistency.</p>
