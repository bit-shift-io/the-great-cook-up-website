a:T59a,<h1>Beef and Mushroom Soup</h1>
<p>Serves 10?</p>
<p>Time: 30min prep + 2hr cook </p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>2Tbl sesame oil</li>
<li>1kg diced beef</li>
<li>2x leek</li>
<li>4tsp chilli</li>
<li>4tsp garlic paste</li>
<li>2Tbl ginger</li>
<li>1 can shiitake murshrooms (~200g)</li>
<li>8 cups chicken stock (would like to try beef stock next time?)</li>
<li>4 cups water</li>
<li>2Tbl soy sauce (we put extra on when we serve so might want more here?)</li>
<li>2x carrot</li>
<li>~200g bean sprouts</li>
<li>300g mashroom pieces</li>
<li>bunch (~8) spring onions</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>Thinly slice leek (pale section only). Dice shiitake mushrooms. Cut carrot into mathsticks (maybe grate next time?). Splice up spring onions</li>
<li>Brown beef in seasame oil, then remove</li>
<li>Heat remaning seasme oil, add leek, chilli, garlic, ginger and cook for around 2 min or till soft. </li>
<li>Add beef and shiitake mushrooms, cook for ~1min</li>
<li>Add stock + water, bring to boil then reduce to low simmer</li>
<li>Cook for 2hrs or till beef tender</li>
<li>Shred beef using 2 forks on a breadboard</li>
<li>Add beef, soy sauce, carrots, bean sprouts, spring onion, mushroom pieces. Cook for ~5min</li>
</ol>
<h2>Notes</h2>
