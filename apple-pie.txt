a:T44a,<h1>Apple Pie</h1>
<p>Serves 4</p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>2Tb butter</li>
<li>2 apples peeled and cubed</li>
<li>1/4 cup brown sugar</li>
<li>1tsp cinnamon</li>
<li>1/4tsp salt</li>
<li>1/2 lemon juice</li>
<li>plain flour</li>
<li>egg wash or milk</li>
<li>puff pastry</li>
</ul>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>Saltanas/rasins</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>Preheat oven to 200c</li>
<li>Medium heat add butter, apples, cinnamon, sugar, salt and lemon till apples are soft and juice cooked off ~8m</li>
<li>Let the mix cool</li>
<li>Lightly floured surface roll out puff pastry and divide for 4 rolls</li>
<li>Spoon the apple mix leaving a border. roll up and crimp edges</li>
<li>Add some cuts in the top with scissors or knife</li>
<li>Brush with egg wash or milk and sprinkle with extra cinnamon + sugar</li>
<li>Bake till golden brown about 25m</li>
</ol>
