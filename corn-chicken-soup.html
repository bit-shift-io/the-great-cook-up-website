Chinese Corn Soup with Chicken

serves 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of chicken or veg stock
  • 1 can creamed corn (wifey doesnt like this)
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tb cooking wine
  • 1 tsp ginger minced
  • 1 tsp garlic minced
  • 1 tsp cornflour/cornstarch
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup diced/shredded chicken

    • Extras

  • frozen veg/corn
  • spring onions

    • Method

  • Bring to boil - stock, creamed corn, soy, wine, ginger, garlic, cornflour
  • When boilding, bring temp down to simmer, let thicken
  • Add extras, chicken
  • Turn off, add egg and mix through till it ribbons
