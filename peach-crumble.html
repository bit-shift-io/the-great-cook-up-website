Peach & Blueberry Crumble / Cobbler

Prep: 10 m Cook: 45 m

Fruit Base Ingredients

Crumble Topping Ingredients

Method

  1. Oven to 180C
  2. Drain juice from 1 can. And combine with other can and its juice, with the other fruit ingredients.
  3. In a bowl combine topping ingredients. Mix till crumbly balls are formed.
  4. Spread 1/2 topping over the base and stir into the fruit.
  5. Spread the remaining topping over the fruit, dont mix in.
  6. Sprinkle with extra cinnamon and sugar for golden top.
  7. Cook in oven 180C for 40-50mins or till light brown top.
