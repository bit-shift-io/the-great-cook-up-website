a:T519,<h1>Bean Enchiladas</h1>
<p>Serves: 15</p>
<p>Time: 1.5h prep + cook</p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>2x brown onion</li>
<li>2x red capsicum</li>
<li>2x green capsicum</li>
<li>2x 420g can corn kernels</li>
<li>2x 420g can black beans</li>
<li>4tsp ground cumin</li>
<li>chillipowder/siracha</li>
<li>2x 400g can chopped tomato</li>
<li>20 tortillas</li>
<li>3x 300g salsa jar (medium or hot)</li>
<li>500g (4cups) grated cheese (reduced fat)</li>
<li>2x avacado (optional)</li>
<li>400ml light sour cream</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>Dice vegetables</li>
<li>Brown onion and capy</li>
<li>Add corn, beans, cumin, chilli, tomatoes and simmer for ~5min or till thickens. Turn off when done</li>
<li>Preheat oven to 200C and grease some oven proof dishes (we used 3 dishes)</li>
<li>Wrap the mixture from above into tortillas and place in baking dishes, spoon left overs over the top.</li>
<li>Pour salsa over the top. Add the cheese over the top of that</li>
<li>Bake for ~25min or till heated through ahd cheese browned</li>
</ol>
<p>Serve with some sour cream and avo</p>
<h2>Notes</h2>
<ul>
<li>Origional recipe: <a href="https://www.taste.com.au/recipes/vegetable-black-eye-bean-enchiladas/AiUK0meH">https://www.taste.com.au/recipes/vegetable-black-eye-bean-enchiladas/AiUK0meH</a></li>
</ul>
