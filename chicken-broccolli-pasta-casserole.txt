a:T51c,<h1>Chicken Broccolli Pasta Casserole</h1>
<p>Serves 18</p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>1.5kg Chicken</li>
<li>1kg Wholemeal pasta spirals</li>
<li>5cup Chicken stock</li>
<li>500g Cashews</li>
<li>500g Mixed frozen veg</li>
<li>500g Frozen broccolli</li>
<li>3cup Milk</li>
<li>4tsp Garlic powder</li>
<li>4tsp Paprika</li>
<li>4tsp Cornflour</li>
<li>Salt &amp; Pepper</li>
<li>500g Cheddar cheese (optional?)</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>Dice and brown chicken, season in garlic powder and paprika</li>
<li>Pour pasta into casserole dish and pour over chicken stock &amp; frozen veg. Cook for 15 minutes @ 190 degress</li>
<li>Mix cachews, milk, garlic powder, paprika, cornflour, salt &amp; papper</li>
<li>Add chicken and sauce, mix in. Cook for 15 minutes</li>
<li>Mix again and cook for another 15 minutes</li>
<li>Mix again, cover in cheese. Cook for 20 minutues</li>
</ol>
<h2>Notes</h2>
<p>Needs more liquids - try 10cups chicken stock and 4cups milk. This will help reduce the need to constantly mix (i.e. step 5 might be eliminated)</p>
