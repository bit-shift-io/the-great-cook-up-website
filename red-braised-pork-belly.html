Red Braised Pork Belly

Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 0.5-1 kg pork belly
  • 2 cups yellow rice cooking wine
  • 11/2-2 Tb sugar cubes
  • 3-4 slices ginger
  • 1 Tb dark soy sauce
  • 1-2 tsp salt

    • Method

  • Cut pork belly into 2-3cm cubes
  • Heat oil, medium-high
  • Cook till light brown, then remove from pan
  • Bring cooking wine to boil
  • Add ginger, dark soy, salt and pork
  • Simmer on low heat for ~40m (20m then stir)
  • Add sugar cubes, high heat till it caramelises

    • Notes

    Serve with rice and fried green veg (bok choy) or green salad
