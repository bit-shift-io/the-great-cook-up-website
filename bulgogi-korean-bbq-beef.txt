a:T471,<h1>Bulgogi - Korean BBQ Beef</h1>
<h2>Marinade</h2>
<ul>
<li>3 Tb soy sauce</li>
<li>2 Tb brown sugar [white sugar ok]</li>
<li>1 Tb honey [ 2t sugar is also ok ]</li>
<li>2 Tb rice cooking wine or red wine [mirin works too]</li>
<li>1 Tb sesame oil</li>
<li>2 Tb minced garlic</li>
<li>1 tsp ground black pepper</li>
<li>2 tsp sesame seeds</li>
<li>1 Tb chopped spring onion</li>
<li>1 Tbs kiwi fruit per 0.5kg meat [or 3 Tbs onion puree]</li>
</ul>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>0.5kg Thinly sliced beef (sirloin, top sirloin or any tender loin area)</li>
<li>Onion</li>
<li>Carrot</li>
<li>Mushroom</li>
<li>Capcicum</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>Marinade meat minimum 30m</li>
<li>Stirfry meat till charred</li>
<li>Drain juice to side while cooking, dont overload it needs to sizzle</li>
</ol>
