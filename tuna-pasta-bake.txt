a:T54e,<h1>Tuna/Salmon Pasta Bake</h1>
<p>Serves: 26</p>
<p>Time to make: 20min prep + 20min cook</p>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul>
<li>3x 425g tin Tuna or Red Salmon</li>
<li>2x 500g Wholemeal pasta</li>
<li>1x 500g frozen Cauliflower</li>
<li>1x 500g frozen mixed veg</li>
<li>3x Brown onion</li>
<li>4x 400g tin Cherry Tomato</li>
<li>1x 500g Ricotta (Can hardly taste, try fetta next time?)</li>
<li>120g bag baby Spinach (optional)</li>
<li>4Tbs garlic</li>
<li>$Tbs balsamic vinegar</li>
<li>Sprinkle of basil spice</li>
<li>Parmy (optional)</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>Preheat oven to 200deg. Cook pasta for ~10 min with frozen veg. Drain</li>
<li>Medium heat oil in frying pan. Brown onion, garlic; then add tomato and vinegar</li>
<li>Combine pasta, veg, tomato mix, tuna (keep 50% of juice for extra juice n flavour), spinach in baking dish. Cover in ricotta and parmy (optional). Sprinkle with basil. Bake for ~20min</li>
</ol>
<h2>Notes</h2>
<p>I used Tuna, but Red salmon is given in the origional recipie which might have a stronger flavour.</p>
<p>Serve with a sprinkle of parmy instead of adding it at bake time.</p>
