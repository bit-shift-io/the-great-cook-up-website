a:T5e7,<h1>Vietnamese Noodle Salad</h1>
<p>Serves 4<br>Prep 1 h<br>Cook 10 m  </p>
<h2>Marinade</h2>
<ul>
<li>600-800 g chicken thigh or breast butterflied</li>
<li>2 cloves garlic minced</li>
<li>2 Tb lime juice</li>
<li>2 Tb fish sauce</li>
<li>1 Tb soy or light soy</li>
<li>2 Tb brown sugar</li>
<li>1 Tb veg oil</li>
</ul>
<h2>Sauce</h2>
<ul>
<li>1/4 cup fish sauce</li>
<li>4 Tb rice vinegar</li>
<li>2 Tb white/raw sugar</li>
<li>1/2 cup water</li>
<li>2 garlic cloves minced or finely chopped</li>
<li>3 Tb lime juice</li>
<li>dash chilli (optional)</li>
</ul>
<h2>Noodle Bowl</h2>
<ul>
<li>1/2 packet of 250g vermicelli noodles</li>
<li>2 carrots julienned</li>
<li>2 cucumbers julinned</li>
<li>6 ice berg lettuce leaves finely sliced</li>
<li>crushed nuts</li>
<li>onion or spring onion (optional)</li>
<li>1 cup bean sprouts (optional)</li>
<li>hand full of mint leaves (optional)</li>
</ul>
<h2>Method</h2>
<ol>
<li>Marinade chicken for min 1 hr, overnight best.</li>
<li>Cook chicken on medium heat for 6-8 mins. Cool and slice.</li>
<li>Prep vermicelli noodles according to packet. Rinse with cold water.</li>
<li>Mix sauce. Ensure sugar dissolves.</li>
<li>Prep veggies. </li>
<li>Serve with crushed nuts on top.</li>
</ol>
